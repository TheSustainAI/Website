An origin story

AI that works where your farm does

This started with a farmer, a field, and the decisions that could not wait.

Read the story
My father is a farmer. I grew up watching him walk the fields before sunrise, checking soil by feel, reading the sky for weather, making a hundred small decisions before most people were awake. He did not have sensors or software. He had experience. And most of the time, that was enough.— Deepak Sai Pendyala, CEO

But sometimes it was not. A waterline contamination he caught two days too late. A disease in the herd that could have been flagged a week earlier if the data had been there, and readable, and in his hands when it mattered. The information existed somewhere. It just never reached him in time.

Farms generate more data than ever. But data is not the same as a decision.

Most of this information sits in disconnected systems, in formats that require a specialist to interpret, behind dashboards that assume reliable internet and a desk. Farmers are not sitting at a desk.

Envision this

It is 5:30 in the morning. Your phone buzzes once.

The SustainAI system flagged an overnight shift in water conductivity at the south well. The reading crossed a threshold that, based on your soil type and recent weather, suggests possible contamination from field runoff.

Because the system had a connection last night, it already pulled the latest county water advisory and cross-referenced your readings against it. It is showing you two recommended actions in three sentences.

You did not open a dashboard. You did not call a consultant. You did not wait for a cloud server. The answer was already there, generated on your property, by a system that understands your farm.

How SustainAI works

Three layers. One clear answer.

01

Capture

SustainAI Sense

Connects to existing water, soil, and environmental sensors. Captures and normalizes real-world signals into one place.

02

Reason

SustainAI Hub

On-site intelligence that stores data locally, runs fine-tuned models, builds knowledge graphs, and pulls web data when connected.

03

Act

SustainAI Edge

Delivers short, plain-language recommendations, alerts, and action steps to your phone, tablet, or barn display.

What we have already proven

The architecture is not theoretical. It has been tested.

Our first working system, PASTOR, was built for phosphorus sustainability research. It integrated thousands of scientific papers into a connected knowledge base with citation-grounded AI assistants. That same architecture is what powers SustainAI for agriculture.
